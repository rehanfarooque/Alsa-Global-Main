AlsaGlobal — Real-Time Global Intelligence Dashboard

AlsaGlobal is a real-time global intelligence dashboard. Live news, markets, military movements, conflicts, and infrastructure in one view.

Tracks live conflict events from ACLED and UCDP, satellite fire detection from NASA FIRMS, earthquakes from USGS, aircraft from OpenSky, ship AIS from AISStream, and macro data from FRED, IMF, and BIS.
